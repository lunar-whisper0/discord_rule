Server Rules

Please read carefully. These apply to all channels, voice, DMs initiated via this server, and events.

Be respectful

  • No harassment, hate speech, slurs, or personal attacks.
  • Treat everyone with kindness. Disagree with ideas, not people.
  • Keep debates constructive; avoid political/religious flame wars.
Violations may lead to warnings, mutes, or bans at moderator discretion.

Safety & legality

  • No doxxing, threats, gore, or encouragement of self-harm.
  • No sharing pirated, malicious, or illegal content.
  • Follow Discord Community Guidelines & TOS.
Report dangerous behavior to staff immediately.

Content & channels

  • Use the correct channels; keep threads on topic.
  • No NSFW content of any kind is allowed, in any channel.
  • Tag spoilers and heavy content with appropriate warnings.
Staff may move or delete off-topic content.

Voice & text etiquette

  • No spamming, loud mic noises, or soundboards without consent.
  • Use push-to-talk if requested; respect AFK channels.
  • English (or server language) only unless a channel states otherwise.
Temporary mutes may apply for disturbances.

Self-promo & ads

  • No unsolicited advertising, invites, or DM promotions.
  • Share work in designated channels; follow channel pins.
  • Staff approval required for partnerships or events.
  • Self-promo is only allowed in #self-promo for members level 15+, and only TikTok or Discord links are permitted.
Breaking this may result in link timeouts or bans.

Moderation policy

  • Follow staff instructions promptly; arguing after a decision may escalate penalties.
  • Appeals can be submitted via #mod-mail or form (see pins).
  • Usernames, avatars, and statuses must be appropriate.
We may update rules at any time; changes take effect on posting.

Age Minimum

  • You must be 13 years or older to join this server.
  • Users found to be under 13 will receive a temporary ban until eligible.
This follows Discord's minimum age requirement per their Terms of Service.
